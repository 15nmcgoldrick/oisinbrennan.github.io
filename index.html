The Legend of Zelda: Breath of The Wild is the 19th main game in the story,
The game was released on the 3rd of March, 2017 for the Wii U and the new Nintendo Switch.
The story follows Link as he awakes from a 100 year sleep after falling to Calamity Ganon

TLOZ:BOTW is the highest rated game of 2017 so far (probably will be passed by Mario Odyssey)
because of how wide and detailed the world is and the endless fun and things to do.
